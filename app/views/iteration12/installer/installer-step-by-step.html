Claim the OZEV chargepoint grant for landlords: step by step for installers

Check what you need to do to claim for the electric vehicle chargepoint grant for landlords on the new OZEV portal.

  1. Step 1 Become an OZEV authorised installer

    To claim for the OZEV grants, installers must be authorised by OZEV. This is valid for installers and subcontractors.

    1. Apply to be an OZEV authorised installer

  2. Step 2 Set-up your first-time access to the EV chargepoint grants portal

    Authorised installers will need to create a password to access the portal.

    1. Request a link to access the portal for the first time if you are already an authorised installer

  3. Step 3 Check the installer responsibilities

    Installers must know and follow the grant rules, legal requirements, and safety standards.

    1. Check the list of residential chargepoint models approved
    2. Check the list of commercial chargepoint models approved
    3. View the checklist for safety and legal responsibilities
    4. Check the guidance on how conduct a site survey
    5. Check how to notify the Distribution Network Operator (DNO) of the installation

  4. Step 4 Understand grant for landlords process and requirements

    Installers must know and follow the grant rules, legal requirements, and safety standards.

    Landlords or their property management companies will be responsible for starting the grant application. Installers will be responsible for making the grant claim.

    1. Check the guidance for installers for the landlord grant

  5. and Engage with customers

    Before submitting a claim, installers will need to engage with customers to provide all information necessary for them to make a grant application.

    Installers will need to:

    • • Check and confirm the installation will take place in a private parking area
    • • Conduct a site survey
    • • Check the property electricity capacity
    • • Provide a quote for the EV chargepoint installation
    • • Inform the total number of EV chargepoints
    • • Inform the total number of EV chargepoint sockets that will be installed
    • • Provide the OZEV Unique Installer Number to the customer
    1. Check guidance for landlords
    2. Check guidance for property management companies

  6. Step 5 Ensure evidence of the installation is collected correctly

    Installers will need to take photos of the chargepoints installed. This must be provided as part of the claim submission to prove the installation was completed.

    1. Check what photographs are accepted

  7. Step 6 Prepare and submit the grant claim

    To submit a claim, installers will need to log in to their account.

    Once the customer submits the grant application, it will appear on the installer dashboard.

    1. Sign in to your installer account on the OZEV EV chargepoint grants portal
