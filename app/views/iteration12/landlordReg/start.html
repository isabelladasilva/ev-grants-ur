Apply for the OZEV electric vehicle chargepoint grant for landlords

Use this service to:

  • create an account on the OZEV Electric Vehicle Chargepoint Grants portal
  • apply for the OZEV electric vehicle chargepoint grant for landlords

Who can use this service:

  • Landlords of residential properties
  • Landlords of commercial properties
  • Property management companies acting on behalf of landlords

Before you start

To create an account you will need:

  • to identify your business by providing your Companies House registration number, VAT registration number or Private Registered Provider of Social Housing (PRP) number
  • access to an email address
  • a UK mobile phone with signal, as we will text you a security code

If you are a Property Management company you will also need the information listed above for each client you register.

If you are an installer check how to create an installer account. If you have already set-up your access, you can sign in to the OZEV EV Chargepoint Grants portal using your email and password.

There are different services and guidance available for the Electric Vehicle Homecharge Scheme (EVHS) and Workplace Chargepoint Scheme (WCS).
