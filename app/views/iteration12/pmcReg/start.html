Apply for the OZEV {% if serviceName %} {{ serviceName }} {% endif %}

Use this service to:

  • apply for the Landlord Chargepoint Grant
  • login or manage your account on the OZEV EV Chargepoint Grants portal
  • register as a Property Management company to apply for an EV chargepoint grant on behalf of your clients
  • register as a Landlord to apply for an EV chargepoint grant for your properties

Before you apply

To apply for grant you will first need to register and create an account.

If you are a landlord you will need:

  • your Companies House number, VAT number, Private Registered Provider of Social Housing (PRP) or Charity number
  • an email address
  • a mobile number

If you are a Property Management company you will need:

  • your Companies House number, VAT number, Private Registered Provider of Social Housing (PRP) or Charity number
  • an email address
  • a mobile number
  • the information listed above for each client you register
Start now

You can also register by post.
