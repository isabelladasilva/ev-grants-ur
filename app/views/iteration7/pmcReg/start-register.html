Register for OZEV Electric Vehicle Chargepoint Grants

Use this service to:

  • register for an OZEV Electric Vehicle Chargepoint Grants portal to apply for the Landlord Chargepoint Grant
  • create an account as a property management company to apply for the Landlord Chargepoint Grant on behalf of your clients
  • create an account as a landlord to apply for the Landlord Chargepoint Grant for your properties

Before you start

To register will need:

  • to identify your business by providing your Companies House registration number, VAT registration number, Private Registered Provider of Social Housing (PRP) or Charity number
  • access to an email address
  • a UK mobile phone with signal, as we will text you a security code

If you are a Property Management company you will also need the information listed above for each client you register.

If you already have an account you can sign in to the OZEV Electric Vehicle Chargepoint Grants portal using your email and password.

There are different services and guidance available for the Electric Vehicle Homecharge Scheme (EVHS) and Workplace Chargepoint Scheme (WCS).

Start now

You can also register by post.
