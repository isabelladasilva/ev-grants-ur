Claim the EV chargepoint grant for landlords: step by step for installers

Check what you need to do to claim the Electric vehicle (EV) chargepoint grant for landlords on the OZEV portal.

  1. Step 1 Apply as an OZEV authorised installer

    You must be an OZEV authorised installer to claim this grant.

    If you already have an OZEV authorised installer number you need to get a link to access the OZEV portal.

  2. or Apply to become an authorised OZEV installer

    If you do not have an OZEV installer number you need to apply to be an OZEV authorised installer.

  3. Step 2 Get your first-time access to the OZEV portal

    You need a link to access the OZEV portal for the first time. You also need to create a password.

    1. Request a link to access the portal

  4. Step 3 Check your installer responsibilities

    We expect OZEV authorised installers to follow the grant rules. You need to be aware of the relevant legal requirements.

    You must also consider safety and accessibility needs when installing chargepoints.

    1. Check the list of residential approved chargepoint models
    2. EV landlord chargepoint guidance: for installers

  5. Step 4 Understand the EV landlord grant process and requirements

    Landlords, right to manage companies and property factors start the grant application process and installers are responsible for making the grant claim.

    1. Check the EV landlord chargepoint guidance: for installers

  6. and Engage with customers

    Installers should engage with customers before submitting a claim. This is your opportunity to gather all the necessary information to make a successful grant claim.

    Installers will need to:

    • • check and confirm that the installation will take place in a private parking area
    • • survey the site to check it has sufficient capacity for the number and type of chargepoints that will be installed
    • • confirm the total number of chargepoints that will be installed
    • • confirm the total number chargepoint sockets that will be installed

    Once you are satisfied that you have all the required information and that the site is suitable for the installation, give your OZEV authorised installer number to your customer so that they can submit the grant application.

    1. Check guidance about private parking
    2. Check the guidance for landlords

  7. Step 5 Collect evidence to show the installation is completed correctly

    You need to take photos of the chargepoints you have installed. These will be submitted as part of the claim to prove that the installation has been completed.

    The photos should:

    • • clearly show each chargepoint serial number and model 
    • • be clear and in view of the property 
    • • be a wide-angle view of the parking area if there are many bays  

    Examples of photos you need to provide

    Chargepoint close up

    Chargepoint and the parking space

    A maximum of 10 photos may be uploaded. They should be in JPEG, JPG, BMP, or PDF format and each one can be up to 10MB in size.

  8. Step 6 Prepare and submit the grant claim

    Once your customer has submitted their grant application it will appear on your installer dashboard.

    The installation must be finished before you can complete a claim.

    1. Sign in to your installer account to submit a claim
