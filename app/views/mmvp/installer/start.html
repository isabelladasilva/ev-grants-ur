Manage OZEV electric vehicle chargepoint grants as an installer

Use this service to:

  • sign in using your email and password
  • check your customer grant application
  • submit claims for your customers
  • manage your customers claims

Who can use this service

  • existing OZEV authorised installers can access this service for the first time by requesting a link to create a password
  • new installers must apply to be an OZEV authorised installer before you can use this service
Start now

Before you start

To create an account you will need:

  • the email address you used to register with OZEV
  • your OZEV installer number

You will also need:

  • access to an email address
  • a UK mobile phone with signal, as we will text you a security code
If you are an installer without an account check how to create an installer account. If you have already set-up your access, you can sign in to continue using your email and password.

There are different services and guidance available for the Electric Vehicle Homecharge Scheme (EVHS) and Workplace Chargepoint Scheme (WCS).

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
