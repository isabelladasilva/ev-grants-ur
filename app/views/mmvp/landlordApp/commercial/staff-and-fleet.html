Are the chargepoints only for the use of staff or fleet vehicles?


Only private parking and chargepoints that are (or will be) for the sole use of staff and fleet vehicles, are eligible for cover under this grant.

What are fleet vehicles?

A fleet vehicle is 1 or more vehicles which provide commercial transport for a company, a government agency or another organisation. These vehicles may be owned or leased by the company or owned or leased by their supplier.
