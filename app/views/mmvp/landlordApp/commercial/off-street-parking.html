Does this property have private parking?

What is private parking?

The address where you’re applying to install chargepoints must have private parking. You must own the parking space or have sole legal right to it.

The private parking may be co-located with the property or separate from it.

Where the parking is separate from the property you must be able to demonstrate you have a legal entitlement to the parking space through the provision of Land Registry title deeds.

If you do not own the parking space or have sole legal right to it you cannot apply for the grant. This includes:

  • rented spaces
  • spaces shared with other properties
  • spaces that provide access routes to other properties

We are not able to permit arrangements that involve cables being placed over public land, such as pavements, even if it is temporary.
