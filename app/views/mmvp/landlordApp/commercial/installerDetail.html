Enter your installer's OZEV number

You need your installer’s OZEV number to continue your application for the Electric Vehicle landlord chargepoint grant. Only OZEV authorised installers are eligible to carry out the installation work. This number can only be provided to you by your installer.

If you do not have an OZEV authorised installer you can choose from OZEV's list of authorised installers.

Read Working with an OZEV authorised installer for more information.

I don't know my installer's number

Contact your installer and ask for this information. OZEV authorised installers receive this number when they register with OZEV.
