Does this property have private parking?

What is private parking?

The address where you’re applying to install chargepoints must have private parking. You must own the parking space or have sole legal right to it.

The private parking may be connected the property or separate from it.

You must be able to show you have a legal entitlement to the parking space if it is separate from the property. You may prove this through the provision of Land Registry title deeds.

If you do not own the parking space or have sole legal right to it you cannot apply for the grant. This includes rented spaces.

Cables are not permitted over public land such as pavements, even if the arrangement is temporary.
