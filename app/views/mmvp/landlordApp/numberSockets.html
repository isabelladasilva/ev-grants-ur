How many chargepoint sockets will be installed at this property?

The number of chargepoint sockets may be different from the number of chargepoints. A chargepoint may have a single chargepoint socket or 2 chargepoint sockets (a twin or double socket).

OZEV pays 75% of the cost of buying and installing an EV chargepoint socket, up to £350.

The grant amount is calculated on the number of chargepoint sockets installed.

I don't know the number of chargepoint sockets
Contact your installer to confirm the number of chargepoint sockets that will be installed. It is important that you agree on the number of chargepoint sockets that will be installed.
