Are you responsible for an electric vehicle?

You are responsible for the vehicle if you are:

  • the registered owner of the vehicle
  • leasing the vehicle or have a finance agreement for this vehicle
  • leasing the vehicle as part of a salary sacrifice scheme
  • the nominated user of an employer vehicle
  • leasing a vehicle as part of the Motability scheme
