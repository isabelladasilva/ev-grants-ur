We've confirmed your eligibility with Electric City Installers

Dear J Smith,

Electricity installers will now be able to install your chargepoint.

Before your chargepoint installation begins:

  • You'll need to show your installer proof that you own, lease or are otherwise responsible for the electric vehicle you added to your application.
  • You'll need to get consent from the owner or owners of the property, if you are not the sole freeholder.
  • You'll also need permission from your local authority before the installation can begin.

If you rent or lease the property, you will also need consent from your landlord or freeholder.

Changing installer

You can change your installer at any time before your installation. Once you've chosen a different installer, they will confirm your details with us and you will receive an email to with instructions on how to change the installer on your application. You will not have to complete your eligibility check again.

Cancel your application

You can cancel your application(link) if you decide not to install a chargepoint at your property.
