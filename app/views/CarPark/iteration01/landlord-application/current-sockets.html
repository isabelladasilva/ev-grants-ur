How many EVs will your infrastructure allow you to charge at once?

We want to know how many EVs your infrastructure will allow you to charge in your carpark, before you install any chargepoints.

The grant will pay up £500 for each EV the infrastructure will let you charge, regardless of whether you install the chargepoints now or later.

To get the grant for infrastructure, it will need to charge at least 5 EVs at the same time.


What do we mean by infrastucture?
Infrastructure means all the cabling and ground work right up to any chargepoints, to make sure they are safely and properly connected.
Tell us the number you have agreed with your OZEV approved installer.
