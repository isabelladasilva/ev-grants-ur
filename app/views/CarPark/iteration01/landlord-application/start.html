Apply for OZEV electric vehicle chargepoint grants

Use this service to:

  • create an account
  • apply for an EV chargepoint grant for your properties
  • manage your applications

Who can use this service

  • landlords of residential properties
  • property factors
  • social housing providers
If you are an installer check how to create an installer account. If you have already set-up your access, you can sign in to the OZEV EV chargepoint grants portal using your email and password.

There are different services and guidance available for the Electric Vehicle Homecharge Scheme (EVHS) and Workplace Chargepoint Scheme (WCS).

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
