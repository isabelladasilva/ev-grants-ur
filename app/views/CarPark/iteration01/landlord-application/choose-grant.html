Which type of grant do you want to apply for?


You can apply for a grant for the infrastructure to charge at least 5 EVs and to install at least 1 chargepoint now, in a carpark.

Or, you can apply for a grant for chargepoints only, if the infrastructure is in place.

What do we mean by infrastucture?

Infrastructure means all the cabling and ground work right up to any chargepoints, to make sure they are safely and properly connected.

It is important you follow your OZEV approved installer's recommendation on whether you need infrastructure.
