What items can go towards the total installation cost
  • Cost of the chargepoint unit
  • Electrical components
  • Civil engineering works
  • Labour costs (for installation)
  • Hardware costs
  • Any onsite one-off software purchases, but not ongoing software support or services
  • VAT incurred by the customer
  • Site survey works (only to do with an installation you completed)

Upload a copy of the invoice you gave to your customer

You can only upload one file. If you sent your customer more than one invoice, add a final invoice that includes your total charges.

The document needs to be in JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, XLS, DOC or PDF format and be less than 2MB.
