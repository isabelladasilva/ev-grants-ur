How many EV charging parking spaces will the infrastructure support?

We want to know how many marked parking spaces your infrastructure will support in your car park, before you install any chargepoints.

The grant will pay up to £500 for each EV charging parking space the infrastructure will support, regardless of whether you install the chargepoints now or later.

To get the this grant, you need to put in the infrastructure to support simultaneous charging for at least 5 EV parking spaces and to install at least 1 chargepoint with 1 chargepoint socket.
