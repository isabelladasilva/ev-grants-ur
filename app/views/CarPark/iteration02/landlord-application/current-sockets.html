How many EV charging parking bays will the infrastructure enable?

We want to know how many EV charging parking bays your infrastructure will enable in your car park, before you install any chargepoints.

The grant will pay up £500 for each EV charging parking bay the infrastructure will let you charge, regardless of whether you install the chargepoints now or later.

To get the this grant, you need to put in the infrastructure to enable simultaneous charging for at least 5 EV parking bays, and to install 1 chargepoint with 1 chargepoint socket.
