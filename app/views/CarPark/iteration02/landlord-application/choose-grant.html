Which type of grant do you want to apply for?


You can apply for a grant for the infrastructure to charge at least 5 EVs and to install at least 1 chargepoint in a carpark. This is called the Residential EV car park grant

Or, if the infrastructure is in place and you are just installing chargepoints, you can apply for an EV landlord chargepoint grant (opens in new tab).

What do we mean by infrastructure?

By infrastructure we mean:

  • cabling that runs from the power supply to the chargepoint location
  • electrical systems
  • signage
  • earthing arrangements
  • any onsite one-off software purchases, but not ongoing software support or services
  • grid connections
  • concrete plinths or footings for future chargepoints
  • vehicle barriers
  • upgrades to existing electrical infrastructure in the property
  • labour
  • project management

It is important you follow your OZEV approved installer's recommendation on whether you need infrastructure.
