What items can go towards the total cost of installation
  • Cost of the chargepoint unit
  • Electrical components
  • Civil engineering works
  • Labour costs (for installation)
  • Hardware costs
  • Any onsite one-off software purchases, but not ongoing software support or services
  • VAT incurred by the customer
  • Site survey works (only to do with installation you completed)
