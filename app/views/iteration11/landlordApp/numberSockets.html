How many chargepoint sockets will be installed at this property?

The number of chargepoint sockets may be different from the number of chargepoints. EV chargepoints can be single-socket or dual-socket.

The grant amount is calculated based on the number of chargepoint sockets. OZEV will pay up to £350 for each socket installed.

I don't know the number of chargepoint sockets
If you do not know or are not sure of the number of chargepoint sockets that will be installed, you need to contact your installer to confirm this information.
